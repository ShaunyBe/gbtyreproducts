About GB Tyre Products

GB Tyre Products was founded and established in Port Elizabeth in 1997 by Gerhard, who is the sole owner of the business.

He built the business to what it is today by providing great service and always putting the customers needs first.

He is also the agent for Corghi equipment, providing tyre changes, wheel aligners and balancers, and also Ps2 Fluid, which is a biodegradable water based tyre fluid, for the Eastern Cape.

Other products that are sold, are Mobi jacks and lifts, Gedore spanners and tyre levers, Kendo spanners and PCL inflation accessories.

GB Tyres operates throughout the whole of the Eastern Cape and provides personalised tyre related services.


Services that are offered:

  • Layout of new tyre shops
  • Tyre related accessories
  • Garage Equipment - Installation of 2 and 4 post lifts (Pneumatic)
  • Air-pipe installations
  • Repair and maintenace of all equipment supplied
